Our Waterfowl

Waterfowl include ducks and gooses. These birds are not afraid of cold water; they would play in it all winter if it did not turn into ice. We have a few breeds of ducks and geese.

Geese

Toulouse

Toulouse geese are beautiful birds. The majority of their feathers are a mix of brown and gray. Their back is white, contrasting with the rest of their body. Males and females both share those characteristics. These geese are noisy and will start screaming if they see anyone coming. They are also extremely protective. They will try to bite you if they see you collect their eggs. However, they can be more docile if you raise them as gooseling.

Pros:

  • They will alert if they see any intruders or predators
  • Produce large eggs

Cons:

  • Noisy
  • They preferably need a water source big enough to swim

Embden

Embden is one of the most common goose breeds. Both females and males are entirely white. Like the Toulouse breed, they are protective and will start screaming. They are efficient alarm systems. In spring 2024, our male goose named Trompette started screaming when a fox was near the farm. Thanks to him, we could react, and no animal was hurt. Embden geese can also be more docile if raised as a duckling. Feet and Foot are two geese we had as babies. They are way less aggressive towards us than their friends we had as adults.

Pros:

  • They will alert if they see any intruders or predators
  • Produce large eggs

Cons:

  • Noisy
  • They preferably need a water source big enough to swim

Ducks

Call Duck

Call ducks are the smallest ducks we have on our farm. Those cute ducks come in multiple color varieties. Currently, we have three gray call ducks. Males have feathers in different shades of gray and a distinctive green head. Females are brown. They look like a smaller version of the Mallard duck. Call ducks can escape since they can fly.

Pros:

  • Beautiful ducks with various color varieties
  • Small

Cons:

  • They can fly
  • They preferably need a water source big enough to swim

Khaki Campbell

Khaki Campbells are the best breed for egg production. They produce a large number of eggs. The females are light brown, and the males are also light brown, but they have black feathers on their backs and a green head. We have one male and a few females, but they do not let themselves be approached.

Pros:

  • Best breed for eggs

Cons:

  • They preferably need a water source big enough to swim.

Indian Runner

Indian Runner ducks are one of the most unique domestic duck breeds. Their bodies and walking style are different from those of most ducks. Their bodies look like those of penguins, and they have a unique walking style. They can come in various colors. We only have one Indian Runner duck named Arthur.

Pros:

  • Multiple color varieties
  • Unique
  • They can not fly

Cons:

  • It is not the prettiest breed of ducks
  • They preferably need a water source big enough to swim
