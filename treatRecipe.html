Our treat recipe

We often give our flock of chickens our special treat. Most of them run toward us when they see us coming with a cauldron. We feed them food that will boost their health while spoiling them with tasty treats. Many of the vegetables and spices we use are grown in our garden.

Base Ingredient

  • Rice or spaghetti

Herbal Tea Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Parsley
  • Oregano
  • Mint
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary

Extra Ingredients

  • Sardine can
  • Corn kernel
  • Cooked carrot
  • Banana (but not the peel)
  • Watermelon
  • Pumpkin and other squash

Steps

  1. Cook the rice or the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a cauldron of water, heat water until it boils. Once it boils, turn off the heat and add all the herbal tea ingredients.
  3. Mix the pasta and herbal tea and let it cool down. If there is too much herbal tea for the amount of pasta, you can store the extra herbal tea in the fridge for the next time.
  4. Add any elements of the extra ingredients. Our chickens love corn kernels and sardines.
