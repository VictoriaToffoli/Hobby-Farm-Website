About Us

We are a small family with a big love for animals. In 2021, we bought four chickens with a small coop. By the end of the same summer, we had 20 chickens and a few quails. Since then, our farm just kept expanding. Today, our hobby farm includes chickens, ducks, geese, pheasants, guinea fowl, partridge, and a few mammals. On this website, we want to share our experiences, and what we have learned, so it can inspire others for their hobby farm.
